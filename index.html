About Us.

Advocate Jose Joseph, with over 40 years of legal expertise, stands as a distinguished figure in the legal community. Specializing in civil and criminal law, as well as legal drafting, he has carved a niche for himself through his extensive experience and commitment to justice. Beyond his practice, Adv. Jose Joseph has held significant positions in various government entities. He currently serves as a Board Member of the Kerala Water Authority, reflecting his deep involvement in public service. From 2011 to 2016, he contributed as a Board Member of the Kerala Minorities Finance Development Corporation, showcasing his dedication to societal development. His legal career is marked by notable milestones, including his tenure as the Government Pleader in Mannarkkad Court from 1991 to 1996.



His leadership is further evidenced by his election as the President of the Mannarkkad Bar Association for the second time, a position he continues to hold with great honor. In addition to his legal work, Adv. Jose Joseph is actively involved in social and political activities. He serves as the State General Secretary of the Kerala Congress (M) party, highlighting his influence in the political arena. His expertise is widely recognized by nationalized banks, including SBI, Canara Bank, and Dhanalaxmi Bank, as well as cooperative banks and KSFE, where he serves as a legal panel advocate. As a pioneer in legal scrutiny reports and a state notary, his contributions are indispensable in property verification and documentation. Advocate Jose Joseph's vast experience across diverse legal and governmental domains equips him to tackle every legal challenge with unmatched precision and dedication.

Adv. Thomas Paul
